Data Sources
Our program data is gathered by a small team of researchers who have searched the internet for popular programs in the city. Our search methods involve visiting local college program websites such as by Columbia, NYU, Rockefeller, Cornell, Princeton, Baruch, CCNY, Cooper Union, Vanderbilt etc. Many of these programs have been ones that past Stuyvesant students have participated in, thus adding to the catalog. Another source is by gathering lab/research internships for the Intel Regeneron Competition. Many of these students attend summer labs for which these programs have been searched and designed for. Another source we use is our the opportunities page which goes around regularly by Mr. Blumn. For programs submitted via users, we do validate and make sure that those program details are accurate. If you notice any typo/discrepency, please contact us via the email box at the bottom of the landing page. We hope you will appreciate this accumlation of data, and to give credit to all the program hosters and sources from which this data was compiled.
Technical Errors
Sometimes links are updated or break. In the event that you notice any typo/link break or any other technical errror, it would be appreciated if you could report that to our team. You can use the "Contact Us" box on the landing page to do so. Please use the subject header: "TECHNICAL ISSUE" This will help us sort through our emails. Back to Landing Page
